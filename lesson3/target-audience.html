Targeted Audience:

The main targets for this website range from local people just wanting to know to days weather and to people traveling in the area who planning trips for friends, family or paid tours. So, this site will be a great resource for any person who can operate a computer, laptop, tablet or any mobile device (ages 10+).


Persona 1:

  • Age: 28
  • Gender: Male
  • Relationship Status: Single
  • Education: College Graduate
  • Special Considerations: Planning a Trip for some buddy's and wants to keep up with changing weather patterns.

Persona 2:

  • Age: 56
  • Gender: Female
  • Relationship Status: Married,3 Adult Children
  • Education:High School Graduate
  • Special Considerations: She has 3 dogs and want to keep up with the weather, so she does not get rained on taking them for a walk.

Scenarios:

  1. What will the weather be later today?
  2. Will I need a coat for the football game tonight?
  3. Do I need to put chains on my tries today?
  4. How many days are predicted to be sunny?
  5. How much snow should I be expecting to see in the next couple of days?

Site Name:

Action Weather of Franklin County


Site Purpose:

The main purpose of this website is to bring up to date weather with lighting speed to people living in and traveling to Franklin County, Idaho. This site will also contain information about three places, in particular, Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven and what weather you can expect to see from yearly weather statistics. Also, there will be a place to post patrons' stunning and wonderful pictures of this breath-taking area.

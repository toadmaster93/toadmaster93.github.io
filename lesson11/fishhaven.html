Fishing and Boating Convention

by Matt Anderson
Come this weekend to the Town of Fish Haven, were they will be hosting their annual Fishing and Boating Convention. This great event brings together the best of the Southern Idaho to our amazing shores to experience both our incredible fishing opportunity and the latest and greatest in fishing equipment. This event also has other amazing activities, from who can catch the biggest bass, to the mouth watering fish fry on Saturday night. There are also many more outdoor and indoor events. I guess you will have to find out the rest when you come down this weekend.

Wait I hear you Dads, you’re having trouble convincing the wife this is a great thing to take the family to this weekend. We also offer kid friendly activities, from making their own lures, making their own amazing fishing hats, and free lessons on the basics of fishing. For the miss’s we have amazing cooking demonstrations and classes, to a jewelry market.

If you can’t wait to see what other events we have or to see what is free you can check out our website for all event information.
